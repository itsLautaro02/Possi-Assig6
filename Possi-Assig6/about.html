About Me

Lautaro Possi – Computer Science Student

National Louis University | Chicago, IL

I’m someone who’s always been curious about how technology works behind the scenes. From building small apps in high school to learning more advanced coding concepts in college, I enjoy the process of creating something that actually works and helps people.

I currently study Computer Science at National Louis University while also working in the restaurant industry. Balancing both has helped me build discipline, communication, and problem-solving skills that I apply to everything I do.

Outside of school and work, I like exploring new tech, playing soccer, and working on creative projects like designing concepts and short videos. My goal is to keep growing as a developer and eventually build tools and apps that make everyday life easier.
